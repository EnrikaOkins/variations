TELL ME

          Tell me if you ever cared,

if a single thought

                            for me was spared.

       Tell me when you lie in bed,

do you think of something

                                        I once said.

             Tell me if you hurt at all,

when someone says

                          my name with yours.

      It may have been so long ago,

but i would give

                              the world to know.

-Lang Leav
